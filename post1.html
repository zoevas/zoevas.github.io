My Journey into Semantic Engineering

I started as a software engineer fascinated by how systems think, structure knowledge, and represent meaning.

Over time, I discovered semantic technologies, ontologies, and AI systems that go beyond simple code — they model concepts, relationships, and understanding.

This shift changed how I design software. Instead of just building features, I began designing meaning-driven systems.

Today, my work lives at the intersection of software, AI, and semantics — where logic meets creativity.
